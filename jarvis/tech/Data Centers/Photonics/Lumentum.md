## Overview
Lumentum designs and manufactures active photonics components and subsystems used in optical communications and sensing. Typical offerings include lasers (DFB, tunable), modulators, photonic integrated circuits (PICs), optical engines, and optical subsystems used by transceiver and module manufacturers as well as OEMs in telecom and data center markets. They also have product lines for lidar and industrial sensing.

## Products & technologies
- Laser diodes (DFB, tunable, and specialty lasers)
- Optical modulators and photonic integrated circuits (PICs)
- Optical engines and subassemblies for building transceivers and modules
- Packaging, test, and opto-electronic integration services

## Customers & markets
- Cloud and hyperscale data centers (for short- and long-reach interconnects)
- Telecom carriers and equipment vendors
- Module and transceiver integrators
- Industrial and automotive customers for sensing/LiDAR

## Role in the photonics supply chain
Lumentum is primarily a midstream component supplier: it takes wafers or substrates (often from upstream substrate suppliers and foundries), manufactures lasers and PICs, packages them, and supplies those active components to module assemblers and OEMs.

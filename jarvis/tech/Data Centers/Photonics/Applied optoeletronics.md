## Overview
Applied Optoelectronics designs and manufactures fiber-optic transceivers, breakout modules, active optical cables, and optical subsystems used in short-, medium-, and long-reach data center interconnects. Their specialization is high-volume module assembly, testing, and supply to cloud/hyperscale customers and telecom carriers.

## Products & technologies
- Pluggable transceivers (e.g., QSFP, OSFP style modules) at multiple speeds (100G, 200G, 400G and beyond)
- Active optical cables (AOCs) and fiber assemblies
- Subsystem integration, testing, and qualification services for optical modules

## Customers & markets
- Hyperscale cloud providers and data centers (primary buyers of high-volume transceivers)
- Telecom carriers and network operators
- System integrators and OEMs that require turnkey optical modules

## Role in the photonics supply chain
Applied is a downstream module manufacturer and integrator. They typically source active components (lasers, photodiodes, modulators) and sometimes subassemblies from component suppliers (like Lumentum), plus substrates/epitaxy indirectly from upstream suppliers (like AXT). Applied focuses on high-volume mechanical assembly, optical alignment, testing, and qualification for end customers.

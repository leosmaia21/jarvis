## Overview
Marvell Technology designs and develops semiconductor solutions for data centers, enterprise networking, and storage. In photonics, they provide optical transceivers, DSPs for coherent optics, and integrated solutions for high-speed data transmission in cloud and telecom networks. Their photonics portfolio includes 100G/400G/800G modules, PAM-4 DSPs, and silicon photonics for AI and HPC interconnects.

## Products & technologies
- Optical transceivers: QSFP-DD, OSFP modules for data center interconnects.
- DSPs and ASICs: For coherent and direct-detect optics, enabling long-reach transmission.
- Silicon photonics: Integrated photonic circuits for high-bandwidth applications.
- Networking solutions: Ethernet switches with optical interfaces.

## Customers & markets
- Hyperscale data centers (e.g., for AI training and inference).
- Telecom carriers for 5G and optical transport.
- Enterprise and cloud providers needing high-speed optics.

## Role in the photonics supply chain
Marvell sits midstream, providing both optical components and integrated systems. They source lasers and modulators from suppliers like Lumentum, integrate with their ASICs, and supply finished modules to OEMs and data centers. Competes with Broadcom and Intel in optical silicon.

## History & ownership
Founded in 1995, spun off from Intel in 2000. HQ in Wilmington, DE. Acquired optical companies like Inphi and Innovium to build photonics expertise.

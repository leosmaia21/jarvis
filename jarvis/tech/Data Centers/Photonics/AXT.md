## Overview
AXT is primarily an upstream materials supplier that manufactures and supplies specialized semiconductor substrates and epi-ready wafers used by photonics and RF/power device manufacturers. These substrates are the raw material on which epitaxial layers are grown and active devices (lasers, photodetectors, modulators) are fabricated.

## Products & technologies
- Epitaxial-ready substrates (InP, GaAs, GaN, and other compound semiconductor substrates)
- Engineered substrate products for optoelectronics and high-frequency / power applications
- Custom substrate services and wafer processing support for customers

## Customers & markets
- Laser and photonics component manufacturers (like Lumentum and other laser/PIC vendors)
- RF and power device manufacturers that use GaN/GaAs
- Research and smaller photonics fabs that require specialty substrates

## Role in the photonics supply chain
AXT sits upstream as a materials supplier. They provide the physical substrate that is used by epitaxy houses and device manufacturers. Without reliable, high-quality substrates from companies like AXT, downstream component makers cannot produce lasers, modulators, or PICs at scale.
